# OctoAcme Project Management Overview

## Executive Summary

OctoAcme is a forward-thinking organization that scales institutional knowledge through structured project management processes and collaborative tools like Copilot Spaces. This document provides a brief overview of the key project management processes that enable OctoAcme teams to deliver successful projects efficiently.

## Core Principles

OctoAcme's project management approach is grounded in five core principles:

1. **Customer-First Delivery** - Prioritize customer value and outcomes in every decision
2. **Iterative Releases** - Deliver value incrementally with regular feedback cycles
3. **Clear Ownership** - Define distinct roles and responsibilities for all team members
4. **Data-Informed Decisions** - Use metrics and evidence to guide project direction
5. **Continuous Improvement** - Learn from every project and build institutional knowledge

## Project Management Phases

### 1. Initiation
Validate business needs, confirm measurable outcomes, align stakeholders, and create a lightweight Project One-pager that captures the project's purpose, goals, and success criteria.

### 2. Planning
Break work into shippable increments, identify dependencies and risks, define acceptance criteria and definition of done, and align timelines with clear responsibilities.

### 3. Execution & Tracking
Manage day-to-day delivery through:
- Daily standups for quick synchronization
- Weekly syncs for progress tracking and blocker resolution
- Sprint-based workflows using project boards for visibility

### 4. Risk Management & Communication
- Maintain risk registers reviewed weekly
- Identify stakeholder communication needs and cadence
- Escalate blockers appropriately to unblock teams
- Regular stakeholder updates (monthly or as needed)

### 5. Release & Deployment
Standardize the process for moving features to production:
- Quality gates and testing requirements
- Rollout strategies to minimize risk
- Observability and monitoring post-deployment

### 6. Retrospective & Continuous Improvement
Capture learnings from each sprint, release, or milestone through blameless retrospectives that focus on 2-3 actionable improvements for the next cycle.

### 7. Roles & Responsibilities
Clear definition and collaboration between key personas:
- **Project Managers** - Coordinate delivery, manage timelines, and escalate blockers
- **Product Managers** - Define outcomes, prioritize work, and represent customer needs
- **Developers** - Implement solutions with quality focus and participate in planning
- **QA** - Ensure quality through comprehensive testing strategies

## Key Features

- **Structured Roles**: Each team member understands their responsibilities and dependencies
- **Regular Communication Cadence**: Weekly PM + Product Manager syncs, twice-weekly standups, monthly stakeholder updates
- **Quality & Testing**: Unit tests, integration tests, end-to-end smoke tests, security scanning, and manual QA
- **Risk Visibility**: Continuous risk management with live registers and clear escalation paths
- **Continuous Improvement**: Blameless retrospectives focused on actionable improvements

## Scaling Institutional Knowledge with Copilot Spaces

OctoAcme uses Copilot Spaces to centralize and scale institutional knowledge by:
